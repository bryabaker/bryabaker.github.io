ARTIST STATEMENT WORKS CONTACT

Bryant Baker

Also known As Bryker

As a digital media artist my works center on the interconnectivity between people technology and nature. The process that helps me create these works is one of examination where the focus is on the wide spread effect of a topic all the while keeping the micro and macro in mind. I want to do more than just highlight the connection but focus the work on a point where the point of contact and impacts are felt by the viewer over their process. My media includes digital processes completed with the help of programs like Photoshop, Maya, Processing and Illustrator. The challenge I feel when creating in a new space whether digital or physical is one that can be summed up with the question “how is this topic effecting more than itself.” Answering that question in the form of art not only connects me to the work but the work to the world.
